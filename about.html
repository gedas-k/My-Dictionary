Brevity buddy is a program to help you learn military aviation brevity.

Explain - Click 'Hit me!' and you see a random brevity word. Try to explain what does it means in your head and check if you're correct by clicking 'Show/Hide'.

Say - Click 'Hit me!' and you see a random explanation. Try to say brevity and check if you're correct by clicking 'Show/Hide'.

Good luck!
